USD

Old notes and coins are destroyed

Notes in poor condition are collected by banks, sent to their district Federal Reserve Bank and shredded (about 7,000 tons per year). Worn coins are collected and then used in the minting of new coins.
Gold

Loss to technological uses

Gold used in technologies like cell phones is in such small quantities that it does not get recycled and is lost when the technology is discarded.
Bitcoin

Lost bitcoins

Public and private keys of a bitcoin are needed to complete a transaction and if those keys are lost through hard drive destruction, the bitcoin is unusable and permanently out of circulation.
